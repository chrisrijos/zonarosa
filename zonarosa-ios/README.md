# ZonaRosa iOS

ZonaRosa is a free and open source messaging app for simple private communication with friends.

[![Available on the App Store](https://zonarosa.io/external/images/app-store-download-badge.svg)](https://apps.apple.com/app/id874139669)

Also available on [Android](https://github.com/zonarosa/zonarosa-android) and [Desktop](https://github.com/zonarosa/zonarosa-desktop).

## Questions?

For troubleshooting and questions, please visit our [support center](https://support.zonarosa.io/) or [unofficial community forum](https://community.zonarosausers.org/).

## Contributing Bug Reports

The best way to submit a bug report or support request is [via the Support Center](https://support.zonarosa.io/hc/requests/new). ZonaRosa iOS doesn't collect any analytics or telemetry, and we rely on your feedback to help us troubleshoot and fix problems when something isn't working correctly.

## Contributing Code

Instructions for how to configure your development environment and build ZonaRosa iOS can be found in [BUILDING.md](https://github.com/zonarosa/ZonaRosa-iOS/blob/main/BUILDING.md). We also recommend reading the [contribution guidelines](https://github.com/zonarosa/ZonaRosa-iOS/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Contributing Ideas

Have something you want to say about ZonaRosa Foundation projects or want to be part of the conversation? Get involved in the [community forum](https://community.zonarosausers.org).
